Our Signature Services

Elevated Balloon Styling in Winnipeg & Surrounding Areas

At Elan Atelier, we specialize in modern balloon installations designed to create statement focal points for your celebration. Our designs are clean, intentional, and tailored to your event theme and venue space.

Explore our balloon styling services below.

1. Balloon Arch Installations

Custom organic balloon designs built around your color palette and event style.

What We Offer:

  • Single arch balloon installations
  • Double arch layered designs
  • Organic balloon garlands
  • Coordinated 2–4 color palettes
  • Balanced, photo-ready styling

Perfect for: Birthdays, baby showers, bridal showers, engagements, milestone celebrations, and corporate events.

2. Custom Balloon Styling

Designed to transform your venue with a cohesive balloon focal point.

We create:

  • Arch-based statement pieces
  • Cake table balloon accents
  • Floor balloon clusters
  • Entryway balloon features
  • Layered balloon depth for added dimension

Each design is customized based on your venue size and event vision.

3. Specialty Balloon Finishes

Upgrade your design with premium balloon options:

  • Chrome finishes
  • Matte tones
  • Neutral luxury palettes
  • Soft pastel combinations
  • Bold custom color blends

Premium finishes may affect final pricing.

4. Professional Setup & Teardown

We provide a seamless experience from installation to removal.

Our service includes:

  • Delivery to your venue
  • Professional on-site installation
  • Structural safety and clean finishing
  • Dismantling and removal after the event

You focus on your guests — we handle the details.
